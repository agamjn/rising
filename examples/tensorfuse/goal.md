# Goal

**Primary:** Build brand awareness in the self-hosted LLM and MLOps community
so that when an engineering team decides "we need to bring inference in-house",
Tensorfuse is one of the first names they think of.

**Secondary (downstream):** Drive qualified inbound to the website — engineers
who already understand the problem space and recognize Tensorfuse as a credible
option, not cold leads who need to be educated from scratch.

**What success looks like:**
- Recognition: when "self-host LLM" comes up in a Reddit thread, someone in
  the comments mentions us unprompted
- Quality of inbound: weekly inbound from people who've read our content and
  show up with specific architecture questions, not "what does this do"
- Karma / standing: the founder account is recognized as a useful contributor
  in r/LocalLLaMA / r/MachineLearning / r/devops, not a marketer

**What success does NOT look like:**
- Vanity karma from low-effort posts in big general subs
- Direct conversions from cold posts (Reddit isn't a conversion channel for B2B infra)
- Spammy product mentions that get downvoted and damage the account
